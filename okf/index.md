# okfi knowledge catalog

`okfi` is a terminal (ncurses) browser, editor, and PDF exporter for Open Knowledge
Format (OKF) bundles — it roams project to project, with a BBS-style themed interface.

* [OKF format (parser subset)](/okf-format.md) - The OKF v0.1 structure okfi reads and how it parses frontmatter.
* [Bundle discovery](/discovery.md) - How okfi finds bundles across projects, and the placement model it assumes.
* [TUI viewer](/tui-viewer.md) - The browser view: panes, navigation, markdown styling, cross-links.
* [In-TUI editor](/editor.md) - The built-in concept editor: keys, codepoint handling, atomic save.
* [Creating bundles & concepts](/create.md) - Scaffolding new bundles and concepts, in-program or headless.
* [Configuration & settings](/config.md) - The XDG config file, every option, and the settings screen.
* [Build & run](/build.md) - How to compile and launch okfi.
* [PDF export](/pdf-export.md) - Export a concept to a military-style PDF (milstd milmanual) via pdflatex.
